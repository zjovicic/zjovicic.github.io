Artificial Intelligence and Vibe Coding

  1. Sloppy, poor quality projects made with AI, where one should have coded them manually to ensure safety, security, and optimal performance
  2. Making good, useful projects even in technologies one doesn't perfectly know with the help of AI. Discovering endless possibilities of this new technology. I hope my use of AI belongs to this category.
